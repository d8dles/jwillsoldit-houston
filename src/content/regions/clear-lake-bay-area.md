Clear Lake and the Bay Area follow I-45 (the Gulf Freeway) southeast from Houston into Galveston County, with NASA Parkway and SH 146 running along the bay itself. NASA's Johnson Space Center sits on NASA Parkway in the Clear Lake area. Port Houston's Bayport terminal in Pasadena and Barbours Cut terminal in La Porte sit farther southeast along the bay-side port corridor. The Kemah Boardwalk sits on Galveston Bay just south of Seabrook. Housing includes master-planned subdivisions built out over several decades, waterfront townhomes and garden apartments near the lake and bay, and larger acreage lots toward the Galveston County line.
