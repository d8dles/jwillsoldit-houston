Northeast Houston and the Lake Houston area follow US-59/I-69 (the Eastex Freeway) out from Beltway 8, crossing the Grand Parkway (SH 99) toward the Harris County line. Deerbrook Mall sits at the US-59/FM 1960 interchange in Humble. Kingwood and Atascocita sit around Lake Houston; most of Kingwood is in Harris County, with a portion in Montgomery County. Housing includes master-planned subdivisions built out over several decades, wooded acreage lots near the lake, townhomes near the freeway corridor, and newer construction concentrated toward the Grand Parkway.
