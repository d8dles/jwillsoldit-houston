Central Houston covers the area inside and just outside Loop 610, where I-10, I-45, and I-69/US-59 converge near downtown. All of it sits in Harris County. Downtown Houston anchors the district on the north side; the Texas Medical Center sits south along Brays Bayou; the Uptown Houston District, centered on Post Oak Boulevard, sits west near the Loop's edge. Housing spans early-20th-century bungalows in older sections, garden apartments and townhomes from later infill, and high-rise or mid-rise condominiums closer to downtown and Uptown. Street patterns vary block to block rather than following one subdivision plan.
