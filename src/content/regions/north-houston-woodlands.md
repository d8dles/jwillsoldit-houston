North Houston stretches from George Bush Intercontinental Airport up I-45 and the Hardy Toll Road toward The Woodlands and Montgomery County. The Grand Parkway crosses the corridor between them. Housing ranges from established subdivisions and townhouses to newer master-planned sections and larger lots farther north. The Woodlands has its own Township government structure rather than city government, and the broader corridor crosses several school, utility, and taxing boundaries.
