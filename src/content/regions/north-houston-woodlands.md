North Houston and The Woodlands follow I-45 (the North Freeway) and the Hardy Toll Road north from Beltway 8 into Montgomery County, crossing the Grand Parkway (SH 99) along the way. George Bush Intercontinental Airport sits along I-45 north of Beltway 8, in Harris County. Farther north, The Woodlands Town Center is the commercial core of The Woodlands Township, a planned community that spans mostly Montgomery County with a small portion in Harris County. Housing includes master-planned subdivisions and township sections built out over several decades, townhomes near commercial nodes, and larger acreage lots toward the Montgomery County line.
