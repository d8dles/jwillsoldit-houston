Southwest Houston and Fort Bend County follow US-59/I-69 (the Southwest Freeway) and US-90A out from Harris County into Fort Bend County, crossing the Grand Parkway (SH 99), the Westpark Tollway, and the Fort Bend Parkway Toll Road. Sugar Land Town Square, at the center of Sugar Land, functions as the region's civic and commercial hub, with Sugar Land City Hall on its plaza. Housing includes master-planned subdivisions built out over several decades along the freeway corridors, townhomes and garden apartments near employment centers, and newer construction concentrated toward the Grand Parkway.
