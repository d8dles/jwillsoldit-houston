East Houston and Baytown follow the Houston Ship Channel out along I-10, SH 225, and SH 146, from Harris County into Chambers County. Port Houston's Turning Basin Terminal, the navigational head of the Ship Channel, sits east of downtown; the ExxonMobil Baytown Complex, a refining and petrochemical site, sits farther east along the channel; the San Jacinto Monument stands where the channel meets the bay, near La Porte. Housing includes postwar subdivisions in the older, closer-in sections, townhomes near the Ship Channel corridor, and newer construction and acreage lots as the region extends toward Chambers County.
