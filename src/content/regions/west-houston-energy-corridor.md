West Houston and the Energy Corridor run along I-10 (the Katy Freeway) from Beltway 8 out past the Grand Parkway (SH 99). The Energy Corridor District occupies a stretch of I-10 between Kirkwood Road and Barker Cypress Road, with employers concentrated along Eldridge Parkway. CityCentre sits at the I-10/Beltway 8 interchange. The region spans Harris, Fort Bend, and Waller counties, with the Westpark Tollway providing an east-west route south of I-10. Housing includes master-planned subdivisions built out over recent decades, townhomes and garden apartments near employment centers, and condominiums concentrated around CityCentre.
