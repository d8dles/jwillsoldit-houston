Northwest Houston sits along US-290 (the Northwest Freeway) and SH 249 (the Tomball Parkway), between Beltway 8 and the Grand Parkway (SH 99). All of it is in Harris County. Willowbrook Mall sits near the SH 249/FM 1960 interchange; Vintage Park, a retail and office development, sits nearby off SH 249. Housing dates mostly from suburban subdivision building in the 1970s through the 2000s, with townhomes and garden apartments mixed in near the freeways and newer construction concentrated toward the Grand Parkway. Most of the region is unincorporated Harris County, with a few small incorporated cities, such as Jersey Village, inside it.
